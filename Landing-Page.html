WanderLust

The landscape will be so breathtaking we will turn the music off.We will spend every moment on poorly maintained back roads that lead nowhere.Eventually, the van will break down, and we will teach ourselves ballroom dancing while we wait on the tow truck to come. We will have infinite number of new inside jokes. We'll pretend we're outlaws for an afternoon , and spin a tail together of an adventure on the run.
